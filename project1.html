CHEER UP! ATHLETES.

✿[2023]✿
✿Installation-Interaction-Graphic Design✿
♥ TUTOR:SUZY CHAN ♥
✿

在中国严厉的审查之下，酷儿群体被迫隐形，中国政府不允许相关内容在大众眼中常态化。运动员作为中国社会有着极高威望与正面形象的群体，在中国被视为一种国家形象，他们是不被允许带来负面影响的。在这矛盾的二者之间，酷儿运动员身在其中处于一种非常两难的处境，他们要为了国家和自己的事业塑造正面形象，没办法实现真正的自我认同，这是一种十分令人悲哀的现状。该项目将酷儿线索结合酷儿信息讽刺性的与为运动健儿庆祝的行为内容进行结合，传递给那些运动赛场上的酷儿群体，让他们知道自己并不是独自在战斗，生活。有着和他一样的群体在背后支持着他。脱离运动的国家性与政治性，尝试给予他归属感，增强酷儿运动员的自我认同。

✿

Under China’s harsh censorship, the queer community is forced to be invisible. The Chinese government does not allow content related to homosexuality to be normalized in front of the general public. As a group with high prestige and positive images in Chinese society, athletes are regarded as a part of the national image in China. Thus, they are not allowed to have a negative influence on the public. Queer athletes are in a dilemma. Though they want to create a positive image for their country and their career, they are unable to achieve true self-identity, which is a very sad situation.In this project, queer cues and queer messages are satirically combined with the celebrating activities for athletes. This is a message to the queer community in sports arenas that they are not alone in their battles. They are backed up by the whole community. This aims to separate them from the national and political attributes, try to give queer athletes a sense of belonging and enhance their self-identity.
