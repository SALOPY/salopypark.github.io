Under Chinaâ€™s harsh censorship, the queer community is forced to be invisible. The Chinese government does not allow content related to homosexuality to be normalized in front of the general public. As a group with high prestige and positive images in Chinese society, athletes are regarded as a part of the national image in China. Thus, they are not allowed to have a negative influence on the public. Queer athletes are in a dilemma. Though they want to create a positive image for their country and their career, they are unable to achieve true self-identity, which is a very sad situation.In this project, queer cues and queer messages are satirically combined with the celebrating activities for athletes. This is a message to the queer community in sports arenas that they are not alone in their battles. They are backed up by the whole community. This aims to separate them from the national and political attributes, try to give queer athletes a sense of belonging and enhance their self-identity.
